Pizza Margherita

Description

The Margherita pizza is a simple yet iconic Italian pizza featuring a thin, crispy crust topped with fresh tomato sauce, slices of mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil leaves. Lightly drizzled with olive oil, this pizza highlights the beauty of fresh ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella
  • Fresh basil
  • Olive oil, oregano (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare Dough: Roll out pizza dough on a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  2. Add Toppings: Spread tomato sauce, add mozzarella slices, and sprinkle with a pinch of salt.
  3. Bake: Bake at 220°C (425°F) for 10-12 minutes until crust is golden and cheese bubbles.
  4. Finish with Basil: Add fresh basil leaves and drizzle with olive oil before serving.
