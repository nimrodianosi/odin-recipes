Lasagna

Description

A classic Italian layered pasta dish, lasagna is made with sheets of pasta layered with a rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted mozzarella or Parmesan cheese. Baked to golden perfection, this hearty dish is comforting and flavorful.

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Ground beef
  • Onion, garlic, tomato sauce, tomato paste
  • Béchamel sauce, ricotta (optional)
  • Mozzarella, Parmesan
  • Olive oil, salt, pepper, Italien herbs

Steps

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce: Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil, add ground meat, cook until browned. Add tomato sauce, tomato paste, and seasonings. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  2. Layer the Lasagna: Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish, layer pasta sheets, then meat sauce, béchamel, and mozzarella. Repeat layers, ending with béchamel and a sprinkle of Parmesan.
  3. Bake: Cover with foil and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 25 minutes. Uncover and bake for 15 more minutes until golden. Let rest before serving.
