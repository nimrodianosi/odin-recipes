Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu is a classic Italian dessert made by layering coffee-soaked ladyfinger cookies with a creamy mascarpone mixture, flavored with a touch of cocoa powder on top. Light, airy, and deliciously rich, it's the perfect sweet ending to any meal.

Ingredients

  • Ladyfingers
  • Espresso
  • Mascarpone, heavy cream, sugar
  • Cocoa powder, optional dark chocolate savings

Steps

  1. Prepare Cream: Whisk mascarpone with sugar and whipped cream (add egg yolks if desired).
  2. Layer: Dip ladyfingers in espresso and layer in a dish. Spread half the mascarpone mixture, repeat layers.
  3. Chill: Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours.
  4. Serve: Dust with cocoa powder and garnish with chocolate shavings if desired.
